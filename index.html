My Favourite Hobby

Hello Everyone! I'm Parnika, Today I'm going to tell about my hobby Drawing.

Every one of us has some kind of liking/ disliking for certain things in life. Similarly I love drawing because drawing is so much fun.I cannot imagine a colorful life without drawing.
